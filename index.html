The Inspire Ring (for learners age 8 and over) consists of 20 paired descriptions that profile a teacher's Teaching Style. It invites the learners to select the descriptions that best describe their teacher. The Ring helps learners make sense of the teacher's attitude to and relationships with pupils. The Inspire Ring gives immediate feedback to the teacher in the form of a personal chart, allowing a structured and focused discussion of the teacher's strengths and issues for development.

It gives four bits of information:

  • overall inspiration score from 0 to 100%
  • your balance between support and challenge e.g. 60% to 40%
  • a % score for each of the styles
  • your specific strengths and issues

You can create a collated report of a group of learners through ASSEMBLY. See resources for more information.
